Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

UrgeWise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

By using the UrgeWise app or website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Who we are

UrgeWise is an independently developed mobile application for Android, designed to help individuals track and manage body-focused repetitive behaviours (BFRBs).

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@urgewise.app.

Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not store your password in plain text.

User-entered content

The app allows you to log the following types of personal data:

  • Urge and incident entries (including type, location, body part, and description)
  • Thoughts and feelings journal entries
  • Goals and achievements
  • Custom locations and body parts
  • Streak and points history

This data is stored securely in your personal account and is not shared with or visible to other users.

Anonymous usage data

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics data to help us understand how the app is used and improve the user experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain the UrgeWise app and its features
  • To send transactional emails such as account confirmation and password reset (via our email provider, Resend)
  • To analyse anonymous usage patterns and improve the app
  • To respond to support requests sent to our support email

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is protected by row-level security — meaning only your account can read or write your data.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

Third-party services

UrgeWise uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (account confirmation, password reset)
  • Analytics — anonymous, aggregated usage analytics

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@urgewise.app and we will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will permanently delete your data within 30 days.

Children's privacy

UrgeWise is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@urgewise.app and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@urgewise.app.
